What is the SmartFunder Benefit?

SmartFunder is a mechanism for Employers of choice to increase their EVP and assist their employees to pay school fees in a smarter, more tax efficient way. Government created this incentive to include the private sector in its efforts to make education more affordable and accessible in South Africa.

SmartFunder offers an holistic, outsourced solution, assisting companies to set up tax compliant bursary structures, get employees onboarded, verification of educational institutions and payment directly to the educational institutions for optimal tax compliance.
